About Me

For the past four and a half years, I’ve been deeply involved in the music industry, a field that has captivated me since my earliest memories. My love for music is profound, and I’m always seeking to expand my knowledge in every corner of this vast landscape. My musical roots are firmly planted in the rich soils of 90s boom bap and RnB, so I have always admired pioneers like Flipmode Squad, Common, The Alchemist, Mobb Deep, and many others who have left an undeniable mark on the genre.


I’ve spent countless hours mastering digital audio workstations like FL Studio and Logic Pro X. These tools have become an extension of my creative mind, allowing me to bring you tracks that blend unique samples with powerful drums, resulting in instrumentals that are a true reflection of my artistic spirit.


While I started with production, I’ve found that my real passion lies in lyricism. I don’t just make music to cater to the market; I make music that people need. My lyrics are more than just words; they are stories of resilience, love, and introspection that I hope connects with listeners on a deeper level.


In my quiet moments, I carefully craft each line, each word, ensuring they carry the weight of the message I aim to convey. The rhythm, the cadence, the flow - every element is intentional, contributing to the overall narrative.


Beyond being a composer, I see myself as a storyteller. I use music as a medium to convey emotions, to share experiences and to connect with listeners on a level that transcends sound.


As I continue on this musical journey, I draw inspiration from the ordinary and the extraordinary, from everyday life and unique experiences. I hope that one day, my music will touch someone, offering truth, sparking joy, or simply providing a soundtrack to their life. Until then, I’ll keep chasing melodies, seeking truth, and weaving my existence into every note. I am not just creating music people want, I am creating music people need.
