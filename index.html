About Me

For the past four and a half years, I’ve been deeply involved in the music industry, a field that has captivated me since my earliest memories. My love for music is profound, and I’m always seeking to expand my knowledge in every corner of this vast landscape. My musical roots are firmly planted in the rich soils of 90s boom bap and RnB, so I have always admired pioneers like Flipmode Squad, Common, The Alchemist, Mobb Deep, and many others who have left an undeniable mark on the genre.
