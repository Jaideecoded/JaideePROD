Girls & Ganja - Jaidee Ft PassiveTheRapper Prod x JustLayton

Girls and Ganja was a very spontaneous project. While staying with my close friend Passive, I was constantly surrounded by music that popped up from nothing. We could be sitting around, bored with no idea of what we wanted create that night, then next thing you know we would be standing behind eachother waiting to get on the mic. I've learnt that rolling with the first good idea you have is usually the best option otherwise you get wrapped up in too many good ideas, indecisive of the next step. I woke up one moring and realised that I see a lot of similarities between girls and ganja... So I made a track about it.
